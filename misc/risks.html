Technology Risks

Digital simulations and serious gaming are offer potential benefits in terms of student engagement, motivation, and learning outcomes. However, there's a dearth of clear empirical evidence regarding their effectiveness, and thus, the adoption of these tools largely falls on the instructor or institution's discretion. The lack of a comprehensive policy or guidelines for their integration into educational systems is also a challenge.

1. Online Rudeness: The anonymity of the digital world can lead to inappropriate behavior, which can emotionally impact users.

2.Barrier to Physical Play: Digital games and simulations are often sedentary activities ,bad for heath?

3.Addictive Nature of Games: Many games are designed to be addictive.

4. Curriculum Alignment: Not all games are designed with educational goals in mind.
